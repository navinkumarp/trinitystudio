Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the rental?

All our spaces include professional audio system, lighting setup, air conditioning, and comfortable seating. Technical support is available on request.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance, especially for weekends and popular time slots. Last-minute bookings may be available based on availability.

Can I bring my own equipment?

Absolutely! We welcome artists who want to use their own instruments or recording equipment. Our technical team can help with setup and integration.

Is parking available?

Yes, convenient parking is available near the studio. We're also easily accessible by public transport in Kodambakkam.

Ready to Start Creating?

Whether you're hosting a stand-up show, recording a podcast, or organizing a corporate event, Trinity Studio has the space and support you need.
